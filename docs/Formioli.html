Formiolli Fonts

I needed a custom font for a game that I am making, instead of downloading one like a sensible person I just made my own. It only supports english and most common symbols. As you can tell it's eccentric and pixalated, I personaly love it. It can only be scaled in multiples of 16px or it'll be blurry.

I also made a smaller version for the same game. This text supports less symbols, as some symbols have been converted into special symbols specificly for the game. This must also be scaled in multiples of 16px.

If you enjoy these fonts and wish to use them for anything, feel free to pick them up! You can download them below, go nuts.
