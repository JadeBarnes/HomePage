About particle life

Particle Life Simulation

Particle life, sometimes called particle zoo, is a simulation of emergent properties from simple rules. Each particle has a type, which you tell from the color. Each type has a differant attraction or repulsion from every other type. And each type also has a range on it's attraction. With these simple rules that are randomly initialized, we can see complex "lifeforms" emerge from these particles, as groups of them come together acting as a part in the whole.
